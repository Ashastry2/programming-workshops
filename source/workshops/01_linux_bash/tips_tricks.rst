7. Tips and tricks
====================

Customizing your ``.bashrc``
****************************

Brace expansion
********************

.. code-block:: sh

    mkdir {a..c}.txt

Aliasing
********************

.. code-block:: sh

    alias gst="git status"

Processes
********************

.. code-block:: sh

    # SIGKILL
    kill -9 pid
    # SIGTERM
    kill -15 pid
